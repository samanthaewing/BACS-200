Simon Sinek - "How Great Leaders Inspire Action"
  • Simon Sinek has a simple but powerful model for inspirational leadership -- starting with a golden circle and the question: "Why?"
  • His examples include Apple, Martin Luther King Jr. and the Wright brothers ...
  • Simon Sinek explores how leaders can inspire cooperation, trust and change.
Vanessa Van Edwards - "You are Contagious"
  • Vanessa reveals that our behaviour, from hand gestures to how we say 'hello', changes the way others perceive our confidence.
  • Vanessa Van Edwards is an interpersonal intelligence expert and public speaker.
  • She is a monthly columnist for Entrepreneur; the leading instructor in people skills on Udemy and CreativeLive; and has led training sessions at a number of Fortune 500 companies around the world.
Brene' Brown - "The Power of Vulnerability"
  • Looks at the human connection.
  • Studies our ability to empathize, belong, and to love.
  • Deep insight into Brene's research to understand humanity.

Daniel Pink - "Drive: The Surprising Truth About What Motivates Us"
  • Daniel Pink provides concrete examples of how intrinsic motivation functions both at home and in the workplace.
  • More than money as a motivator.
  • Unhitching pay and bonuses from measurable, personal performance.
Ha-Joon Chang - "Economics is for Everyone"
  • Chang explains why every single person can and SHOULD get their head around basic economics.
  • Studies our ability to empathize, belong, and to love. He pulls back the curtain on the often mystifying language of derivatives and quantitative easing, and explains how easily economic myths and assumptions become gospel.
  • Arm yourself with some facts, and get involved in discussions about the fundamentals that underpin our day-to-day lives.
